# Student Fines Management System Features

## Overview
The Student Fines Management system provides students and staff with a comprehensive interface to view, track, and manage their library fines. This system offers detailed insights into fine history, payment status, and provides tools for fine management and payment requests.

## Core Features

### 1. Comprehensive Fines Dashboard
- **Complete Fine History**: Shows all past and current fines with detailed information
- **Payment Status Tracking**: Clear indicators for unpaid, paid, and waived fines
- **Fine Calculations**: Detailed breakdown of how fines were calculated
- **Related Book Information**: Complete book details for each fine
- **Timeline Tracking**: Creation dates, payment dates, and due dates

### 2. Advanced Statistics Overview
- **Total Fines Count**: Lifetime count of all fines incurred
- **Unpaid Fines**: Number and amount of outstanding fines
- **Paid Fines**: Count of successfully settled fines
- **Amount Due**: Current outstanding fine amounts
- **Total Amount**: Lifetime total of all fines
- **Average Fine**: Statistical analysis of fine patterns

### 3. Outstanding Fines Alert System
- **Prominent Warnings**: Clear alerts for users with unpaid fines
- **Borrowing Restrictions**: Information about borrowing limitations
- **Payment Reminders**: Guidance on settling outstanding amounts
- **Contact Information**: Direct links to library assistance

### 4. Powerful Filtering and Search
- **Status Filtering**: Filter by unpaid, paid, or waived status
- **Year Filtering**: View fines by specific years
- **Sort Options**: Multiple sorting criteria (recent, oldest, amount, book title)
- **Pagination**: Efficient handling of large fine histories

### 5. Detailed Fine Information
- **Fine Amount**: Clear display of exact fine amounts in ETB
- **Calculation Details**: Days overdue and rate information
- **Book Context**: Complete book information (title, author, ISBN, category)
- **Borrowing Timeline**: Borrow date, due date, and return date
- **Payment Information**: Payment method and date when applicable
- **Notes and Comments**: Any special notes associated with fines

### 6. Payment Request System
- **Online Payment Requests**: Submit payment requests for unpaid fines
- **Payment Guidance**: Clear instructions on payment procedures
- **Library Integration**: Seamless connection with library payment systems
- **Receipt Tracking**: Information about payment documentation

### 7. Visual Status Indicators
- **Color-Coded Items**: Different visual styles for different payment statuses
- **Status Badges**: Clear indicators for unpaid, paid, and waived fines
- **Amount Highlighting**: Visual emphasis on outstanding amounts
- **Urgency Indicators**: Special styling for high-priority fines
